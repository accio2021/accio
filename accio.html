Accio

Shopkeepers, have you ever been frustrated by inventory managing? Worry not! Accio is here to save the day.
Accio is a device that helps you to count stock left on the shelf. When the stock count is low, the Accio app will send a notification to notify you. You can also send a message to your supplier to order goods via our app!
